\section{Reproducibility And Artifact Availability}

This anonymous manuscript is backed only by the frozen official reportable surface recorded in \texttt{paper\_run\_manifest\_index.json}. Every headline number in the paper maps to a bundle under \texttt{artifacts/tmlr\_official}.

The supporting reproducibility layer includes:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
\item the official run registry in \texttt{paper\_run\_manifest\_index.json};
\item benchmark and provenance summaries in \texttt{DATASET\_MANIFEST.md};
\item a claims-to-artifacts map in \texttt{paper/shared/claims\_to\_artifacts.md};
\item the official statistics bundle in \texttt{artifacts/tmlr\_official/official\_statistics\_v1};
\item the manual FreshQA audit bundle in \texttt{artifacts/tmlr\_official/freshqa\_leakage\_audit\_manual\_v1};
\item the benchmark reliability notes in \texttt{artifacts/tmlr\_official/mquake\_reliability\_note\_v1} and \texttt{artifacts/tmlr\_official/uniedit\_reliability\_note\_v1}.
\end{itemize}

Additional provenance, audit, and configuration details are included in the anonymous supplementary material bundled with the submission package. The manuscript intentionally points only to frozen local artifact roots and does not require any named external archive.
